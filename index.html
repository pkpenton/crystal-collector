Crystal Collector

Fill your bag with crystals, but be careful not to overflow it.

How to play:

You will be given an exact number of crystals to collect at the start of each game.

Add crystals to your bag by clicking a crystal.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.

You win the game by collecting the exact number of crystals indicated. You lose the game if your total score goes above the number indicated.
